Our Heritage

In the 19th century, Juan Cuevas, a Spanish immigrant from Santander, began what would become a family business spanning four generations. In the Pinar del Rio province of Cuba, Juan cultivated tobacco and turned it into a successful enterprise.

His son, Juan Jr., continued and expanded the business until 1959 forced a dramatic change. Years later, in the Cibao Valley of the Dominican Republic, Luis Cuevas Sr., Juan Jr.'s son, carried on the family tradition at Tabacalera Las Lavas.

Trusted manufacturer for premium cigar brands including Gurkha Cigars, Carlos Torano, Sam Leccia, Cigars International, and Thompson Cigar Group. After decades of crafting excellence for others, we now present Casa Cuevas to the world.

Today, Luis Sr. is joined by his son Luis Jr. in the manufacture and sale of premium long filler cigars. After decades of manufacturing world-class cigars for notable brands, they decided to produce and market a high-end premium cigar bearing the Cuevas name, bringing four generations of expertise directly to you.

Crafted with Tradition

Premium Tobacco Selection

We source a wide range of tobacco from 10 countries including Dominican Republic, Nicaragua, Peru, Colombia, Ecuador, Brazil, Mexico, Honduras, United States, and Indonesia. Each leaf is selected for its quality and contribution to our blends.

Quality Assurance

Every cigar is draw-tested for a smooth, consistent experience. We freeze all product before shipping to eliminate beetle incidents, ensuring each cigar arrives in perfect condition to our retailers and customers.

Family Dedication

The Cuevas family is present at Tabacalera Las Lavas every day, attending to our employees and overseeing every step of the process. This hands-on approach ensures the highest standards in every cigar we produce.

Every cigar combines traditional Dominican craftsmanship, carefully sourced premium tobaccos from 10 countries, and a family commitment to quality that spans over a century.

The Connecticut is our mildest offering. It features an Ecuadorian Connecticut wrapper with Nicaraguan binder and Dominican/Nicaraguan fillers, creating a mild to medium profile with subtle spice. It is perfect for those who prefer a smooth, approachable cigar that doesn't overwhelm the palate.

The La Mandarria is our full-strength offering. This limited edition cigar features an Ecuadorean Habano wrapper with our premium filler blend, pig tail construction, and shaggy foot. It delivers full flavor and full body, designed for experienced cigar enthusiasts seeking maximum impact.

Casa Cuevas cigars are manufactured at Tabacalera Las Lavas in Santiago, Dominican Republic, in the Cibao Valley. This region is renowned for producing some of the world's finest tobacco. Our US office and distribution center is located in Miami, Florida at 5100 NW 72nd Avenue, Unit B.

Casa Cuevas cigars should be stored in a humidified environment between 65-75 degrees Fahrenheit with 65-75% relative humidity. A quality cigar humidor is essential for long-term storage. Keep cigars away from direct sunlight and maintain stable temperature and humidity levels to preserve flavor and prevent beetle infestation.
